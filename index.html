What is Abacus ?

Abacus is an instrument used for making the calclation much easier.

Above you can see a working model of an abacus.

How to use this Tool


basic calculation

Enter first and second digit in the given slot and press Ans or A key in your keyBoard.

You can also move the beads either by touching or clicking on them


Flash Cards

This little fun game is used to develop photographic memory of the student.

Tutor clicks on the Flash Games button and quickly shut the display by clicking on show hide button.

student has to answer as soon as the display shuts or he/She loses.

the aim of this website is to demonstrate about working of an abacus and play a few games alongside.
